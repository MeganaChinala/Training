Links

Links are created by the a element tag and then the href attribute. Syntax is as follows.
<a href = "https://www.google.com"> </a>
anything in between the a tags would be the clickable link to the site. Example: click here

There are two types of URLs - absolute and relative.
Absolute - direct path to another domain site (going from my page to google)
Relative - within the same domain or site (on CNN and going to an article within the site)
